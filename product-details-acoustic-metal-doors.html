Product Description

Skyline provides a wide range of Acoustic Metal Doors available with a variety of configuration and vision panel options, tested according to American Standards (UL) & British Standards (BS). Our full range of insulated doors with up to 3 hours fire rating is designed to suit power substations, cold stores, heat transfers applications and other similar locations. The doors are made of galvanized steel sheets or stainless-steel sheets grade 304 (also available SS grade 316 on special customer request). Door leaves are filled with structural honeycomb or mineral wool with vertical stiffener of high density depending on the type of the door.
