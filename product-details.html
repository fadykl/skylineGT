Product Description

This Lay-In Metal Ceiling system is a premium **aluminum alloy** solution designed for high-end commercial and public spaces. Its durable polyester powder coating ensures **washability** and **corrosion resistance**, providing superior performance over traditional materials. It is mounted on standard suspension grids and offers a clean, accessible finish, perfect for antibacterial requirements.

Technical Specifications

Key Features (Lay-In Metal Ceiling / Antibacterial)

Durable and washable **polyester powder finish**.

Wide range of **standard perforation patterns** (including optional wooden patterns).

**Easy access** for service maintenance (lay-in system).

**Robust and easy to clean** surface with antibacterial properties.

High **sound absorption** and excellent sound attenuation.

**Environmentally friendly and recyclable** (EPD certified per ISO 14025).
