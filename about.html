Our Journey: Excellence in Trade

Skyline General Trading was established on the principle of bridging global markets with local expertise. For over a decade, we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing, trading, and distributing high-quality raw materials and specialized products across the region.

Our strength lies in our deep understanding of the supply chain dynamics and our commitment to building **lasting partnerships**. We strive not just to meet, but to **exceed international standards** in every transaction.

Milestones

2010: Foundation

Skyline General Trading is established in Kuwait, focusing initially on regional construction materials.

2014: International Expansion

Secured first major contracts with suppliers in Europe and Asia, significantly expanding product catalog.

2018: Diversification

Expanded into specialized commodities like industrial metals and chemical feedstock.

2023: Technology Integration

Launched a digital supply chain management system to enhance efficiency and transparency for all clients.

Today: Future Focused

Continuing to lead with sustainable practices and innovative trading solutions.
