# Copper & Grain Craft Distillery - Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Visual Language
- **Artisanal Sophistication**: Premium craft aesthetic that honors traditional distilling heritage
- **Warm Industrial**: Rich copper tones balanced with deep charred wood textures
- **Modern Heritage**: Contemporary layouts with classic typography that evokes established distilleries
- **Sensory Richness**: Visual elements that suggest the warmth, depth, and complexity of premium spirits

### Color Palette
- **Primary Copper**: #FFFFFF - Warm copper tones from stills and equipment
- **Charred Oak**: #FFFFFF - Deep brown from barrel char and aging process  
- **Liquid Gold**: #FFFFFF - Amber whiskey color for accents and highlights
- **Cream Label**: #FFFFFF - Aged paper texture for backgrounds and text areas
- **Dark Glass**: #FFFFFF - Premium bottle glass for contrast elements

### Typography
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" - Elegant serif for headings, evokes established whiskey brands
- **Body Font**: "Source Sans Pro" - Clean, readable sans-serif for content
- **Accent Font**: "Cinzel" - Classic serif for special elements and branding

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries & Effects
- **Anime.js**: Smooth scroll animations and element reveals
- **Typed.js**: Typewriter effect for hero taglines and product descriptions
- **Splide.js**: Premium product carousels and image galleries
- **ECharts.js**: Interactive flavor profile visualizations
- **p5.js**: Particle effects for ambient background atmosphere
- **Splitting.js**: Letter-by-letter text animations for headings

### Header Effects
- **Liquid Metal Displacement**: Subtle animated background suggesting the flow of distilled spirits
- **Parallax Copper Shimmer**: Gentle movement of copper-toned elements
- **Typewriter Hero Text**: Main taglines appear with premium typing animation

### Animation & Interaction
- **Copper Glow Hover**: Buttons and interactive elements get warm copper glow on hover
- **Barrel Roll Transitions**: Page transitions that suggest barrel rolling motion
- **Amber Liquid Fill**: Progress bars and loading animations that fill like pouring whiskey
- **Texture Reveals**: Images and content reveal with subtle paper/cork texture overlays

### Background & Atmosphere
- **Consistent Warm Base**: Cream (#F5F5DC) background throughout all pages
- **Copper Accent Shapes**: Geometric copper elements as decorative features
- **Subtle Texture Overlays**: Light paper grain and cork textures for authenticity
- **Atmospheric Lighting**: Warm, golden lighting effects to evoke distillery ambiance

### Layout Principles
- **Premium Spacing**: Generous white space to convey luxury and craftsmanship
- **Asymmetric Balance**: Editorial-style layouts that feel organic yet refined
- **Vertical Rhythm**: Consistent spacing that guides the eye like reading a fine label
- **Content Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy that mirrors the aging process (base to premium)
